About Our Farm

Family-owned poultry farm with a focus on quality and care.

At Bakare Farm, we combine traditional farming values with modern animal welfare practices. Our birds are raised in spacious coops, fed nutritious grains, and monitored daily for health.

We serve families, restaurants, and businesses with fresh eggs, live birds, and poultry products made on the farm.
